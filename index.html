How to Play

📱

Landscape Mode

When the game starts it rotates to landscape. Hold the phone up so the crowd can see the word.

✅

Got It

Your team guessed it? Tap the right side of the screen. In landscape that becomes the top.

⏭️

Skip

Too hard? Tap the left side. In landscape that becomes the bottom. Skipped words don't count.

👤

Solo Mode

One player holds the phone, everyone else gives clues. Race against words or the clock.

⚔️

Versus Mode

2 to 10 players take turns. Each holds the phone for their turn. Highest score wins. Saved to leaderboard.

⏱️

Timer Mode

Race against the clock instead of fixed word count. Set from 30 seconds to 3 minutes.

🔄

Rounds

In Versus mode choose how many rounds. Most points across all rounds wins.
